Description

A meat-prepared soup useful in many recipes.

Ingredients

  • Beef meat
  • Carrots
  • Fine salt
  • Pepper
  • Celery
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Cut carrots and celery, split the onion in half and put it in a heated pan.
  2. Brown it for some minutes.
  3. Add meat, carrots and celery to a pan, add here the onion and let it boil, cooking it for 3 hours at medium heat. Remove the impurities that come on top.
  4. Filter the soup, splitting it from meat and vegetables.
