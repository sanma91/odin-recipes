Lasagna

Description

Famous italian dish made of pasta layers and bolognese sauce

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan, mozzarella, ricotta and eggs

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Cover with foil and bake
  5. Let the lasagna rest before serving
