Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a tipical Italian dish made of pasta sheets filled in between with meat (more specifically Bolognese sauce) and melted cheese, also known as "Besciamella".

Ingredients

For the pasta sheets

  • Three eggs
  • 400g of flavor
  • 400g of cleaned spinach

For the Bolognese sauce

  • 150g of pancetta meat
  • 700g of grinded meat
  • 90g of butter
  • Half a glass of white wine
  • 300g of tomato sauce and 2 spoons on tomato concentrate
  • One of each
    • carrot
    • onion
    • celery
  • coarse salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Heat a pan with olive oil and butter, add cutted onion and let it brown; then add celery and carrot and do the same.
  2. Add pancetta and after a minute the grinded meat; once it changes colour add salt and pepper.
  3. Add white whine, once it evaporates add tomato sauce and the concentrate. Mix and let it boil, then cover and cook it for 2 hours at low heat.
  4. Salt and cook the spinach, dry it, then cut and prepare and the pastry till it's homogeneous.
  5. Spread the pastry and cut in rectangles.
  6. Boil some water, salt it and cook every sheet at once.
  7. Drain the sheet and lay it in the baking pan, then cover it with Besciamella and Bolognese.
  8. Cover it with grated Parmesan and repeat it till you are done with ingredients, with a last sheet of pasta on top.
  9. Cook it at 180° for 45 minutes.
