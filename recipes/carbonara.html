Carbonara

Description

Carbonara was supposedly invented in Rome during WW2 and it's still one of the most iconic dishes in the world.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Yolks egg
  • Black pepper
  • Pecorino
  • Bacon

Steps

  1. Boil the water with salt.
  2. Cut the bacon in slices.
  3. Brown it for 10 minutes in a pan, when the water boils put spaghetti in.
  4. Put the yolks in a cup, add Pecorino and pepper. Mix until it forms a soft cream.
  5. Meanwhile the bacon is ready, put it in a cup keeping the cooking bottom there, at which you will add some water from the spaghetti pan.
  6. Drain the pasta in the previous pan and add the Pecorino/Yolks mix.
  7. Add the bacon and keep mixing; then it's ready to be served, with some Pecorino and pepper on top.
