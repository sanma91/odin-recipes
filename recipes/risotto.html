Sausages Risotto

Description

One of the many variants of another italian classic

Ingredients

  • Carnaroli rice
  • Half a glass of white wine
  • Pepper
  • Shallot
  • Saffron
  • Butter
  • Sausage
  • Meat soup
  • Grated Parmesan

Steps

  1. Prepare the meat soup.
  2. Clean and cut the shallot, do the same with the sausages.
  3. Heat a pan, put the shallot and after some minutes the sausages.Then add the rice.
  4. Let roast the rice, then add the wine:when it evaporates start cooking it by adding hot soup and mixing it. Keep doing it everytime the soup get absorbed.
  5. Add the saffron and mix it. When the rice is cooked whisk it with butter and Parmesan.
