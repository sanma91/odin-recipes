Broccoli pasta

Description

Not the usual pasta recipe

Ingredients

  • Farfalle pasta
  • Broccoli
  • Heavy cream and butter
  • A cup of Parmesan grated cheese
  • Garlic and black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta in a pot with salted water
  2. Cook the broccoli in a pan until bright green
  3. Melt butter in a pan and add the cream
  4. Add the cheese and garlic, stir and add pasta water if the sauce gets thick
  5. Mix it all together
