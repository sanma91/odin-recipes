Asparagus and eggs

Description

Quick and savory breakfast or light lunch

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove finely chopped
  • 8 asparagus
  • 2 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Heat a small skillet, add oil and garlic and stir
  2. Add asparagus and cook till fork-tender
  3. Spread the asparagus and crack the 2 eggs over; cook the eggs until firm
  4. Season with salt and pepper
